Art in HER+SPECTIVE

Celebrate Women's Month and champion gender equality at HER+SPECTIVE 2024, back for its second year. Immerse yourself in the captivating creations of five exceptionally talented women artists, each sharing their unique perspectives and artistic voices. We extend a warm welcome to exhibitors of ALL GENDERS to join this inclusive celebration of creativity and equality.
